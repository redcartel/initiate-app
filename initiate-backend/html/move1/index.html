Movement in D20 Tactics

At the start of each turn, characters who elected to act immediately take their actions in initiative order prior to all normal movement.

Act Immediately

Characters who elected to move prior to their action move after all other characters' immediate actions, but before any other characters' non-immediate actions.

Move Prior to Action

Characters that acted immediately can use their movement in their post-action movement phase. Post-action movement occurs after all other characters' actions.
